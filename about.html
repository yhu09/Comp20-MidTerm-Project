About Us

Founded in Fall 2019, JumboJets is a student-run organization at Tufts University dedicated to providing convenient, eco-friendly vehicles to students.

Our Team

Our staff members are:

  • Undergraduates at Tufts
  • Trained in vehicle maintenance
  • Environmentally conscious
  • Passionate about helping their peers!

Here at JumboJets, we strive to give Tufts students access to safe and fast transportation around campus.
